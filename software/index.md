## OpenEP

ECCI works closely with the OpenEP Research Laboratory at the Univesrity of Edinburgh. OpenEP and EP Workbench software provides a practical solution for the collection and storage of electrophysiological data recorded using different acquisition machines. For more information visit the <a href="https://openep.org">OpenEP Research Laboratory</a> webpages.
